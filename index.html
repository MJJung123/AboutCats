The top three largest domesticated cat breeds...



Maine Coon

The Maine Coon, also known as the “Gentle Giant”, is the largest breed of the domesticated cats. These large cats have a heavy and silky coat of fur that can vary in many different coat patterns. Their personalities are very amiable, gentle, and dog-like.

Measurments:

  • Height: 10-16 in.
  • Weight: 10-18 lbs.
  • Length: 19-20 in.

Ragdoll

Originating from California, USA, Ragdoll cats are known for their long silky fur and large blue eyes. They are the ideal lap cats because of their easygoing, social, and cuddly nature. They are the second largest domesticated cat breed after the Maine Coon.

Measurments:

  • Height: 9-11 in.
  • Weight: 20+ lbs.
  • Length: 40+ in.

Intelligence 350 degrees.

Playfulness 350 degrees.

Shedding 350 degrees.




Norwegian Forest Cat

The Norwegian forest cat is a stunning breed with a wild beauty and friendly personality. Originally cross-bred with wildcats in Norway, they are now perfectly domesticated, playful, intelligent, and loyal pets. With their muscular bodies, long hair, and striking yellow eyes, they make for a wonderful companion.

Measurments:

  • Height: 9-12 in.
  • Weight: 18+ lbs.
  • Length: 36+ in.

Intelligence 350 degrees.

Playfulness 350 degrees.

Shedding 350 degrees.
